## 随着...的发展

<!-- notecardId: 1765271382323 -->

```tex

```

+ with the progress of technology: 随着科技的发展
+ with the progress of civilization: 随着文明的发展

## ... 正是 ... 的原因

<!-- notecardId: 1765271602355 -->

```tex
It is the ... of ... that ...
```

+ It is the growth/advancement of education that is fundamentally enhancing the intellectual capcacity of the populace: 教育提升是每个人智力发展的原因
+ It is the deficiency of rescourse that is leading to widespread decline in well-being public everyone: 正是资源的匮乏导致了每个人福祉的下降
+ It is the relentless pursuit of economic growth that is primarily responsible for destroy the enviroment globally: 对经济增长不断增长的追求是破坏全球环境的主要原因
